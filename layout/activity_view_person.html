Person Types
Owners, Managers, and Supervisors will receive notifications about Employee activities.

READ
This person can view the designated asset

WRITE
This person can change the designated asset

ALL
This person has permissions for all assets

Assigned Locations
If this person is assigned to a Shift at a certain Location, they will see that Location.

Assigned Messages
If this person is assigned to a Shift at a certain Location, they will see Messages for that Location.

Assigned People
If this Person is assigned to a Shift at a certain Location, they will see all People that have a Shift at the same Location.

Assigned Shifts
This person can only see their own Shifts.

Assigned Time Punches
This person can only see their own Time Punches.

Assigned Inspections
If this Person is assigned to a Shift at a certain Location, they will see Inspection Plans and Completed Inspections at that Location.

Assigned Supplies
If this Person is assigned to a Shift at a certain Location, they will see Supplies assigned to that Location.

Assigned SDS
If this Person is assigned to a Shift at a certain Location, they will see SDS Sheets for Supplies assigned to that Location.

Assigned Tasks
If this Person is assigned to a Shift, they will see Task Templates assigned to that Shift. They will only see Completed and In Progress Tasks that they initiated.
